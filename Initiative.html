Reducing Emissions – Together

"It's important that we take action to reduce the impact of pollution in the city we love. Rydr's mission is to improve the quality of air we breathe as we ride. You can contribute in reducing carbon emission by booking a ride with us. Rydr's mission is to improve air quality by offering sustainable transportation options. By sharing rides or using electric vehicles, we can significantly reduce carbon footprint and create a healthier environment for everyone. Every Rydr ride booked removes CO2 emissions, making a real difference in our city."

How We’re Reducing Emissions

All Rydr rides are carbon neutral – we offset every gram of CO2 emitted via Using Electric vehicles

Select Rydr to reduce emissions for your ride – electric bike cabs, every time

For every ride taken, we plant a tree. This not only helps in offsetting any minimal emissions produced but also contributes to reforestation efforts and biodiversity.

Planting Trees With Every Ride

At Rydr, we are dedicated to supporting air quality improvement and various other projects across Delhi. Our aim is to enhance lives by planting trees throughout the city. According to Trees for Cities, vegetation helps remove dangerous air pollutants, including carbon dioxide, dust, and other gaseous toxins. Together, we can do even more to reduce airborne pollution in the city we live in and love, creating a brighter future for all.
