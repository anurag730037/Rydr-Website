Our Mission

Reducing Emissions- Together

it's important that we take action to reduce the impact of pollution in the city we love. Rydr's mission is to improve the quality of air we breathe as we ride. you can contribute in reducing carbon emission by booking a ride with us.

> know more

Redefining mobility by empowering Men & Women

Rydr is more than just a ride sharing platform; it’s a movement dedicated to providing safe, reliable and empowering transportation for women. Our 100% electric vehicles fleet, driven by women for women and by men for all, ensures a secure, comfortable and green journey every time.


Creating green employment opportunities

Empowering man and women who form part of large work force to become self dependent by rendering green ride sharing services

Know Us Better

Rydr is all about meeting instant needs just in time. Want to catch your metro on time? Or like to send your package across Delhi? Yes! That is all that Rydr is about. We are a power-packed initiative by DMRC in association with AutoPe Solutions, aiming to make your metro life easier.

A service that exclusively stands for the people of Delhi NCR. Rydr is an intelligent bike service that aims to foster affordable, punctual and hygiene-based commuting around NCR. Moreover, it is a one-stop destination that makes speedy delivery and bike rides to the metro swift and accessible.
